Why Faro dominates this list

Three of the five best rooftop bars in the Algarve are in Faro. The reason is the old town: low-rise historic buildings create genuine height without needing a tower block, and the Ria Formosa gives every view something exceptional to look at. Most Algarve tourists skip Faro entirely. That's their loss and your advantage.

Lagos has rooftops too, but the town's layout makes good views harder to find. Albufeira has the Strip — avoid it. The B Rooftop at Hotel Baltum is the honest exception, above the noise and with an ocean view worth making the trip.

Rooftop bars in Faro: the full evening

Do Faro's rooftops as a sequence and you get the best rooftop evening in the Algarve:

Start at LAB Terrace before sunset. Arrive 30–45 minutes early for a west-facing seat, order the €5 house white, and watch the Ria Formosa turn gold. This is the reason people come to Faro rooftops at all.

Move to Cidade Velha Rooftop when LAB closes at 8pm. Five minutes' walk into the old town — cocktails with the cathedral tower and terracotta roofscape lit up around you.

Finish at Terrazzo Rooftop by the marina if the night still has legs — a more polished cocktail list and marina lights on the water.

Three rooftops, one evening, all on foot. No other Algarve town can do that.

FAQ

Are Algarve rooftop bars open in winter?
LAB Terrace in Faro and The Argo in Vilamoura are year-round. Hotel rooftops like B Rooftop may close October–March. Always check before making a special trip.
What should I wear?
Smart casual for The Argo. Everything else is relaxed — clean trainers are fine. The Algarve is not a dress-code destination outside 5-star hotel bars.
Which has the best views?
LAB Terrace for the Ria Formosa panorama at sunset. Cidade Velha for Faro old town roofscape. The Argo for marina views with the best cocktails. Depends what you're after.
How many rooftop bars are there in Faro?
Three worth your time: LAB Terrace (sunset, closes 8pm), Cidade Velha Rooftop (old town views, open later), and Terrazzo Rooftop (marina views). The best rooftop scene in the Algarve, all within a ten-minute walk.
What is the best rooftop bar in Faro?
LAB Terrace — the best sunset in Faro, wine from €5, rated 4.7/5 across 1,100+ reviews. After it closes at 8pm, Cidade Velha and Terrazzo carry the night.
Is Faro good for rooftop bars?
The best in the Algarve. The low-rise old town and the Ria Formosa lagoon give Faro's rooftops views no other Algarve town can match.
Which Faro rooftops are open after sunset?
Cidade Velha Rooftop and Terrazzo Rooftop. LAB Terrace closes around 8pm — sunset there first, then move on.
