Sky Bar Carvoeiro:
The Best View in Carvoeiro, Full Stop

📍 Tivoli Carvoeiro, Estrada do Farol 5 📞 +351 282 351 100 💶 €€€€ · Cocktails €13–15 ⭐ 4.5 / 5 (680 reviews)

Perched on the cliffs above Praia de Carvoeiro, the Tivoli's rooftop bar delivers panoramic views of the Atlantic coastline that are among the most impressive in the Algarve. The cocktails are polished, the loungers are comfortable, and the setting makes the price feel justified. Open to non-hotel guests — book ahead for sunset.

Why I'd send you here

The Algarve has many places claiming the best view. Sky Bar Carvoeiro is one of the few that can actually make the case. The Tivoli sits elevated on the cliff edge — the coastline stretches in both directions, the sea fills the rest, and on a clear evening the light on those ochre limestone cliffs goes through phases that make every other bar setting seem slightly grey by comparison.

The bar itself is well-run. This isn't a case of a spectacular location propping up a mediocre operation — the cocktails are made properly, the service is efficient without being robotic, and the food menu (tapas, sushi, finger food) holds up. You can make a full evening of it.

The lounge setup — sunbeds, low tables, ambient music that doesn't compete with the view — is genuinely relaxing. Unlike some hotel rooftops that feel like they're trying too hard, Sky Bar has the confidence to let the location do most of the work.

The honest catch

It's seasonal. The bar closes in winter and reopens in late spring — check before making the trip outside June to September. Their Instagram @skybarcarvoeiro posts opening announcements each year.

Prices are hotel rooftop prices. A cocktail is €13–15. Champagne is on the menu for a reason. If this is your budget splurge of the trip, it's a good one. If you're watching spend, Cocktail Garden two kilometres away does excellent drinks at half the price.

Sunset tables book out fast in July and August. Walk-in after 8pm is usually fine for a drink at the bar, but if you want a prime table facing the sunset, you need a reservation.

What to order

  • 1
    Sky Bar Signature Cocktail
    The house cocktail changes by season — usually built around local citrus, Portuguese spirits, or fresh herbs from the kitchen garden. This is the one to start with.
  • 2
    Champagne or Cava
    If there's anywhere in the Algarve that earns a glass of fizz, this is it. The view is the occasion. Budget for it.
  • 3
    Sushi or tapas plate
    If you're staying through sunset into the evening, order something to eat. The sushi bar is a serious operation for a hotel rooftop — it's not an afterthought.

Who it's for

  • Best view in Carvoeiro? Unambiguously yes.
  • Budget drinks? No. Come knowing it's expensive.
  • Non-hotel guests? Yes — publicly accessible.
  • Open year-round? No. Seasonal. Check Instagram first.
  • Special occasion? Yes — this is the move for a birthday or anniversary sunset.

How to get there

Follow signs for the Tivoli Carvoeiro Algarve Resort on the Estrada do Farol. The hotel is on the cliff edge west of Carvoeiro beach — there's parking at the hotel. The rooftop bar is accessed through the hotel lobby or via the pool area. Ask at reception if it's not immediately obvious.

FAQ

Do I need to be a hotel guest?
No — it's open to the public. Walk in or book a table. In summer, booking is strongly advised for sunset hours.
How much are drinks?
Cocktails €13–15. Champagne and premium options go higher. Budget accordingly.
Is it open year-round?
No — seasonal. Typically late spring to early autumn. Check @skybarcarvoeiro on Instagram or call the Tivoli before visiting outside June–September.
What's the view like?
Panoramic. The Carvoeiro cliffs, the Atlantic coastline in both directions, and the sea. One of the most photographed views in the Algarve for a reason.
