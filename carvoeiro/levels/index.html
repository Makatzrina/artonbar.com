Levels Carvoeiro:
Three Floors on the Best Square in the Village

📍 Largo da Praia 8, Carvoeiro 🌐 levelscarvoeiro.com 💶 €€ · Cocktails €10–13 ⭐ 4.2 / 5 (560 reviews)

Right on Largo da Praia — Carvoeiro's compact beach square — with three floors, ocean views from level two, cocktails, a wine list that takes Portuguese producers seriously, and live music in season. The natural choice for extending a beach day into an evening without moving far.

Why I'd send you here

Carvoeiro beach is small and beautiful. Largo da Praia surrounds it on three sides. Levels is on that square, which means you can go from sand to cocktail in about ninety seconds. For a beach evening that turns into an evening-evening, this is the most frictionless option in the village.

The three-floor setup gives you options. Ground floor for bar energy and easy ordering. Level two for ocean views and a table where you can have a conversation. Upper level for fresh air and the widest view over the coastline. The right floor depends on what kind of evening you're having.

The wine list is a genuine reason to come. Most beach bars in the Algarve treat wine as an afterthought — a few recognisable labels, all overpriced. Levels stocks regional Algarve producers alongside mainstream Portuguese wines, and the staff can usually tell you something about what's on the list. That's above average for a beach-square bar anywhere.

The honest catch

Service is inconsistent — good when it's quiet, stretched thin when the square fills up in peak season. The 4.2 rating reflects this variability. Go in with reasonable expectations: it's a popular beach bar, not a cocktail lounge with four staff per ten covers.

The food menu is fine without being remarkable. The octopus is frequently praised in reviews. If you're eating here, treat it as beach food — good for what it is, not a restaurant destination.

In July and August the square gets very full. If you want the ocean-view table on level two, get there before 7pm.

What to order

  • 1
    Portuguese wine by the glass
    Ask what's open. The list has regional Algarve wines and interesting Portuguese bottles. This is where Levels earns its distinction from the other beach-square options.
  • 2
    House cocktail
    The cocktail list is solid without being exceptional. The tropical-leaning drinks work well in the beach setting — something with rum and passion fruit is the natural call.
  • 3
    Octopus appetiser
    If you're eating: this is what the regulars order. Simple, local, done well.

Who it's for

  • Beach-to-bar transition? Yes — unbeatable location for this.
  • Ocean views? Yes, from level two.
  • Live music? In season — check their website.
  • Serious cocktail bar? No — go to Cocktail Garden for that.
  • Wine focused? Yes — better Portuguese wine list than most beach bars.

How to get there

Largo da Praia is the beach square at the bottom of Carvoeiro village — you can't miss it. Levels is right on the square, next to the beach. Walk down from the car park above the village (a 5-minute walk) or take the road all the way to the beach level.

FAQ

Which floor is best?
Level two for ocean views. Ground floor for bar energy. Upper level for fresh air and the widest view.
Is there live music?
Yes, regularly in summer. Check levelscarvoeiro.com or Facebook for the schedule.
Is the wine list worth it?
Yes — one of the more serious wine lists in Carvoeiro. Portuguese wines are well represented, including regional Algarve producers.
Is it open year-round?
Broadly yes, reduced hours in winter. Check levelscarvoeiro.com for current times.
