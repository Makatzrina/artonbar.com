Lagos bar etiquette, in case nobody told you

  • Reservations matter at Forbidden Door. Don't show up at 8 on a Saturday with five people and expect a table.
  • Cash is still surprisingly common. ATMs are everywhere, but bring some.
  • Service charge isn't automatic. Tip 10% if you liked it.
  • Most bars don't do food past 10pm. Eat first.
  • The walk between old town and the marina is 15 minutes. Bolt is €4. Both fine.

FAQ

Are Lagos bars open in winter?
Most of the ones on this list, yes. Beach bars close. Old town stays alive.
How late is "late" in Lagos?
Most cocktail bars close at midnight or 1am. Zanzibar and Alma Lounge run till 2–4am.
Is Lagos walkable at night?
Old town, completely. Bring a jumper in winter, the cobblestones get cold.
Card or cash?
Most take cards now. Skybar is cash only. Bring €50 just in case.
