Best Bars in the Algarve
Complete Ranked Guide — 2026

The best bars in the Algarve — overall top 5

1

Forbidden Door — Lagos

Best Overall Craft Cocktails Speakeasy Year-Round

The best craft cocktail bar in the Algarve. Hidden entrance, small-batch spirits, a list built by people who know what they're doing. The 4.5-star rating is earned on the quality of the drinks, not the novelty of the entrance. If you drink one serious cocktail in the Algarve, drink it here.

The Argo — Vilamoura

Award-Winning Marina Views Premium Vilamoura

The most awarded bar in the Algarve, on Vilamoura's marina. Award-winning cocktails, exceptional service, a setting that justifies the premium pricing. The bar for a special occasion, an anniversary dinner that becomes a late night, or the one time you want the full luxury Algarve experience.

📍 Vilamoura Marina 💶 €€€€ · Cocktails €14–20 ⭐ 4.7 / 5

3

Cocktail Garden — Carvoeiro

Craft Cocktails Garden Setting Freestyle Bartending Best in Carvoeiro

Luís runs the Cocktail Garden with a freestyle approach — tell him what you like, he makes something. The outdoor garden setting is one of the most pleasant drinking environments in the Algarve. This is the craft bar that surprises people: they expect a decent beach bar and find something genuinely good.

📍 Urbanização Monte Carvoeiro, Carvoeiro 💶 €€ · Cocktails €10–13 ⭐ 4.4 / 5

4

Arcada Cocktail Club — Tavira

Historic Square Cocktails Wine List Best in Tavira

On one of the most beautiful squares in Portugal with cocktails that match the setting. Tavira is the Algarve that most visitors don't get to — quieter, more genuinely Portuguese, with a bar culture that reflects it. Arcada is the bar that makes the trip worth making.

📍 Praça da República 8, Tavira 💶 €€ · Cocktails €10–14 ⭐ 4.4 / 5

Sky Bar — Carvoeiro

Best Views Hotel Rooftop Seasonal Premium

The best view of any bar in the Algarve — rooftop of the Tivoli hotel above the cliffs, panoramic Atlantic. Seasonal (check before visiting in winter), premium prices, but a setting that earns it. The Algarve at its most photogenic, with a drink in hand.

FAQ

What is the best bar in the Algarve?
Forbidden Door Lagos for pure cocktail quality. The Argo Vilamoura for the full premium experience. Sky Bar Carvoeiro for views. Arcada Tavira for location. It depends on what you want.
Which town has the best bar scene?
Lagos for craft cocktails. Vilamoura for premium. Tavira for authenticity. Portimão for options on a single strip. Carvoeiro for beach-town quality.
What should I drink in the Algarve?
Medronho (local arbutus brandy) is worth trying once. Algarve DOC wines are genuinely good at bars that stock them. Caipirinha is the beach bar standard and consistently well-made.
How much do cocktails cost in the Algarve?
€6–8 at Absolutely Portimão (best value) up to €15 at Sky Bar Carvoeiro (most premium). Most mid-range bars charge €9–13.
