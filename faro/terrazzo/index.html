Terrazzo Rooftop, Faro:
The Polished One

📍 Faro marina area 💶 €€ · Cocktail list ⭐ 4.3 / 5 🌙 Open after sunset

The most composed cocktail list of Faro's three rooftops, with the marina lights below. Not the sunset spot — the spot for the drink you actually think about, later in the evening.

Why I'd send you here

Faro's rooftop scene has a clean division of labour. LAB Terrace owns sunset. Cidade Velha Rooftop owns the old-town atmosphere. Terrazzo owns the cocktail list — the most polished drinks of the three, on a rooftop by the marina with the boats and water below.

It's also the flexible one: later hours than LAB, easier to get a table than either of the others in high summer, and close enough to both that it slots into any Faro evening as the second or third stop.

The Faro rooftop crawl

Sunset at LAB Terrace (arrive 45 minutes early), old-town cocktails at Cidade Velha after 8pm, and Terrazzo for the last, best drink of the night. Three rooftops, all on foot, no other Algarve town can match it — the full sequence is in our rooftop bars guide.

Who it's for

  • A properly made cocktail in Faro? This is the rooftop for it.
  • Marina views? Yes — boats and water rather than roofscape or lagoon.
  • Sunset? Go to LAB Terrace first, come here after.
  • When the other two are full? Terrazzo is the most likely to have a table.

FAQ

Where is it?
Near Faro marina, minutes from the old town. Directions and hours at terrazzobar.pt.
Open late?
Later than the sunset rooftops. With Cidade Velha, it's one of the two Faro rooftops that carry the night after 8pm.
Terrazzo or Cidade Velha?
Terrazzo for the better cocktails and marina views; Cidade Velha for old-town atmosphere. They're minutes apart — do both.
Is it expensive?
€€ — Faro prices. A composed cocktail here costs what a mediocre one costs in the resort towns.
