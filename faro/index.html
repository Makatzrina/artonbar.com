Why Faro is underrated

Faro has an old quarter that holds its own against anything in Lagos, a lagoon out front that turns gold every evening, and a fraction of the tourist load. The bar scene is quieter, but that's part of the point. You pay less, you wait less, you hear the person you came with.

The downside: the bar scene closes earlier. This is a first-drinks town, not an after-midnight town. Plan accordingly — start here, continue somewhere else if you want to stay out.

FAQ

What time do Faro bars close?
LAB Terrace closes at 7:30–8pm. Most cocktail bars go till midnight. It's not a late-night city.
Is Faro worth it for bars?
Yes — especially for sunset and early evening. Cheaper than Lagos, fewer crowds, genuinely beautiful setting.
Are Faro bars open in winter?
Most are. LAB Terrace runs year-round, which puts it above most beach-focused spots.
