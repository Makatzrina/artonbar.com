LAB Terrace, Faro:
Where to Watch the Sun Go Down

📍 R. Cmde. Francisco Manuel, Faro ⏰ Daily 3pm–8pm 💶 €€ · Wine from €5 ⭐ 4.7 / 5 (1,124 reviews) 📞 +351 919 224 112

Faro's best sunset from a rooftop most tourists miss. Wine is five euros, the Ria Formosa stretches out in front of you, and nobody tries to upsell you. Arrive at 6, leave when the lights come on.

Why I'd send you here

Faro is the most underrated town in the Algarve. It has an old quarter that holds its own against anything in Lagos, a lagoon out front that turns gold every evening, and a fraction of the tourist load. LAB Terrace sits on top of all of that.

It's a rooftop. The seats face west over the Ria Formosa. A glass of decent white wine is five euros. The staff are quick, friendly, and don't try to upsell you. The crowd is half locals, half travellers who know.

I've watched the sunset here a dozen times and the only thing I've ever wished for is bar snacks. They don't really do them. Eat before, drink here, eat after.

The catch

The view is partially crossed by overhead train lines. Yes, it's slightly annoying. No, it doesn't ruin anything. After thirty seconds your eyes register the lagoon, the birds, the boats — and the lines stop existing.

The other thing: it closes early. By Faro standards, painfully early. 7:30 to 8pm depending on the season. This is a sunset bar, not a night bar. Plan accordingly.

Who it's for

  • Sunset? Yes. The reason the bar exists.
  • First drink of the night? Perfect. Arrive 6, leave when they close.
  • Date? Yes, especially the first one.
  • Group up to 4? No problem. Bigger — arrive earlier.
  • Quiet conversation? Yes, music stays low.
  • Late-night drinks? No. Try Cidade Velha Rooftop after this.

How to get there

Walk from Faro old town: ten minutes. Walk from the marina: five. Don't drive — parking nearby is limited. Arrive 30 minutes before sunset to get a west-facing seat. In summer, 45 minutes.

LAB Terrace Faro Menu & Prices

The menu is short and honest — this is a drinks-and-view bar, not a restaurant. Prices are the best of any rooftop in the Algarve:

White / rosé wine (glass)€5
Cocktails€8–12
Beer€4–5
Soft drinks€3–4
FoodMinimal — eat before or after

Compare that with €14–17 cocktails at the cliff bars further west and you understand why LAB Terrace fills up. The house white at €5, drunk facing the Ria Formosa at golden hour, is the single best-value drink in the Algarve.

FAQ

When does LAB Terrace close?
3pm to 7:30pm most days, 8pm Friday–Sunday. Sunset focused — don't arrive late expecting it to be open.
Is LAB Terrace open in winter?
Yes, year-round.
Do they take cards?
Yes.
Are there bar snacks?
Almost none. This is the main complaint. Eat first, drink here, eat after.
What's the best time to arrive for sunset?
30–45 minutes before sunset for a west-facing seat — around 7:15pm in midsummer, 5pm in winter. Clear evenings fill fastest.
Is LAB Terrace a rooftop bar?
Yes — Faro's best-known rooftop, open-air, facing west over the Ria Formosa. It tops our rooftop bars guide.
How much does it cost?
Wine from €5, cocktails €8–12, beer €4–5. The best-value rooftop in the Algarve.
Where exactly is it?
Rua Comandante Francisco Manuel, between the old town and the marina — ten minutes from the old town, five from the marina.
