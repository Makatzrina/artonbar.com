For Bar Owners

Want your bar on ArtOnBar?

If you run a bar in the Algarve, here's how it works. No fluff.

The honest version

  • I write the review. You don't.
  • The review will be honest. If your bar isn't great, I won't pretend it is. I'll tell you privately what's not working and we can revisit it later.
  • Featured listings show at the top of their town page with a "Featured" tag.
  • You can also pay for a single sponsored post about a new opening or event. It'll be marked sponsored.
  • You can't pay to remove an honest review or change a star rating.

What you get

  • A real review on the site
  • Photos (mine, or yours if they're good)
  • Map pin on your town hub page
  • Inclusion in relevant pillar guides
  • Newsletter mention when your listing goes live

What I need from you

  • Bar name, address, opening hours, contact
  • Three things you want people to try
  • Best night to visit, worst night to visit
  • A free hour for me to actually come drink there

I usually reply within 48 hours.
