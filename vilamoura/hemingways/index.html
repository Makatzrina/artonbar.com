Hemingway's Bar, Vilamoura:
The Marina's Oldest Institution

📍 Marina de Vilamoura, Vilamoura ⏰ Daily, noon–2am 💶 €€€ · Cocktails €12–15 ⭐ 4.3 / 5 (740 reviews)

Open since the late 1980s, which in Vilamoura terms is ancient history. Marina-facing terrace, a crowd that's been coming back for decades, and drinks that justify the marina markup. The one bar in Vilamoura with actual character.

Why I'd send you here

Vilamoura is a purpose-built resort. It has no old town, no centuries-old tavern, no accidental bar that opened because someone needed a living. Everything was planned. Hemingway's is the closest thing the marina has to something that grew organically — it opened before most of Vilamoura's current development existed, and it's still standing because it does what it does well.

The terrace faces the marina. You'll be watching boats that cost more than most people's houses while drinking something properly made. The crowd is a mix of long-stay expats, resort regulars, and visitors who looked up more than the first result on Google. None of that is bad company.

The atmosphere is the main reason to come. After 30-plus years, the bar has an ease to it. Service is efficient, the pace is right, and nobody tries to upsell you into a bottle package.

The honest catch

Drinks are marina-expensive. A gin and tonic will run €12–14. The food is fine but it's not the reason to be here — eat somewhere else, drink here.

In peak season (July–August), the best terrace tables fill up early. Arrive before 6pm if you want a seat facing the water. After 8pm on a Saturday in high summer, you're managing expectations.

It's also worth saying plainly: Vilamoura is not where you go if you want authentic Portugal. Hemingway's is the best bar in a very resort-shaped town. If character and authenticity matter more to you than marina views, go to Faro or Lagos instead.

What to order

  • 1
    House Gin & Tonic
    They've been making these for 30 years. Well-balanced, proper glassware, the right amount of ice. This is what the bar does best.
  • 2
    Aperol Spritz or Negroni
    Marina classics. Both done properly here. Good for the golden hour when the light hits the water.
  • 3
    Skip "signature" cocktails with the bar's name in them
    Usually just a dressed-up mojito at a premium. Order something off the main menu instead.

Who it's for

  • Sunset marina drink? Yes. This is exactly what it's built for.
  • Something with history? Yes, by Vilamoura standards.
  • Cheap drinks? No. Nothing on the marina is cheap.
  • Quiet conversation? Yes, especially afternoons. Evenings get busier.
  • Late night? It runs till 2am, but the casino bar takes over after midnight if you want to keep going.

How to get there

Right on the marina boardwalk — hard to miss. Walkable from essentially everywhere in Vilamoura if you're staying near the centre. No parking considerations needed; leave the car at the hotel.

FAQ

Is Hemingway's Bar open in winter?
Yes, year-round. Winter is significantly quieter — sometimes it's just you and the regulars, which is its own kind of good.
How much are drinks at Hemingway's Bar?
Cocktails €12–15, beer €5–7. Marina prices. Budget accordingly.
Do I need to book?
Walk-in is fine most of the time. In high season, arrive before 6pm for the best terrace spots. No reservations needed for bar seating.
Is there live music?
Occasionally, especially in summer. Check their Facebook page for events.
