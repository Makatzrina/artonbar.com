The Argo, Vilamoura:
The Best Cocktail Bar in Vilamoura by a Significant Margin

📍 Marina de Vilamoura, Tivoli Hotel (1st floor) ⏰ Daily, 4pm–midnight 💶 €€€€ · Cocktails ~€14–20 🏆 2-Star Iberian Peninsula Top Cocktail Bars

Two stars in the Top Cocktail Bars of the Iberian Peninsula. Best Hotel Bar at the Lisbon Bar Show 2024. Best Hotel Bar at the Condé Nast Traveler Hotel & Mantel Awards 2023. These aren't marketing claims — they're peer recognition in a category where most competitors are better at decor than cocktails. The Argo happens to be good at both.

Why I'd send you here

Vilamoura is designed, not grown. It has no old town, no accidental bar that opened because someone couldn't afford to leave. Everything here was planned and polished. Which makes The Argo's achievement more impressive: it's earned genuine industry credibility in a manufactured setting.

The cocktails are built around the mythology of the Argo — the ship of Jason and the Argonauts — and the drinks back up the concept. The menu is organised by character: Heracles inspires the strength-forward, spirit-led cocktails. Athena's are precise and balanced. Poseidon's carry the sea, salinity, oceanic notes. It's a clever structure that makes ordering easier, which is exactly what a good cocktail menu should do.

The terrace looks over the marina. In the golden hour before dinner, with a well-made drink in hand and the light hitting the boats, there are few better places to be in Vilamoura.

The honest catch

It's inside a five-star hotel. Prices match. A cocktail will likely run €14–20. The space is polished to the point of corporate, which is the Tivoli's character more than The Argo's failing — the bar team clearly care about what they're making, even if the lobby downstairs feels like a conference centre.

If you want a bar that feels scruffy and discovered, go to Faro or Lagos. The Argo is the choice for when the occasion requires the setting.

What to order

  • 1
    Mythology-Themed Signatures
    Tell the bartender whether you're in the mood for something bold (Heracles), precise (Athena), or with sea-forward flavours (Poseidon). They'll point you right.
  • 2
    Cocktail Tasting Menu
    If available that evening, worth doing. A progression through the mythology and flavour profiles. More expensive but the right way to see what this bar does.
  • 3
    Skip the safe choices
    A G&T is fine anywhere in Vilamoura. This bar earns its reputation on the originals. Order something off the themed menu.

Who it's for

  • Special occasion cocktails? Yes. Probably the best setting in Vilamoura for that.
  • Staying at the Tivoli? Obvious choice — you're already there.
  • Not staying at the Tivoli? Walk over anyway. Fully public.
  • Cheap drinks? No. This is a five-star hotel bar. Budget accordingly.
  • Sunset drink before dinner? Yes. Arrive at 5:30pm, leave for dinner at 8.

How to get there

Inside the Tivoli Marina Vilamoura Hotel, first floor. On the marina boardwalk — walkable from essentially anywhere in Vilamoura. No need for a car. Reservations via argovilamoura.pt ↗.

FAQ

Is The Argo open in winter?
Yes, year-round, daily 4pm to midnight.
How much are cocktails at The Argo?
Approximately €14–20. Hotel bar pricing at a 5-star resort. The quality justifies it.
Do I need to book?
For terrace tables in summer, yes. Book via argovilamoura.pt. Bar seating is usually walk-in.
Is The Argo open to non-hotel guests?
Completely open to the public. No membership, no dress code beyond smart casual.
