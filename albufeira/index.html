The honest picture

Most of Albufeira is what gives the Algarve a bad name. Strip-bar tourism, neon, beer in buckets, music nobody chose to play. The bars on this list are the exception.

The two front-row tables at Sal Rosa are gold. Reserve them or arrive at 4pm. If you're showing up at 8pm on a Friday hoping to wing it, you'll be disappointed — and you'll blame the bar when really you just should have planned ahead.

FAQ

Is Albufeira worth visiting for bars?
Yes, if you know where to go. Sal Rosa alone is worth the trip. Avoid the neon strip and you'll have a good time.
Do I need to book Sal Rosa?
For a sunset table, absolutely. For a 4pm drink, no.
When is Sal Rosa closed?
Tuesdays. Open Wednesday to Monday, 4pm to 11:30pm.

Things to do in Albufeira

Benagil cave tours, sunset sailing, coastline boat trips — activities to pair with the bars.
